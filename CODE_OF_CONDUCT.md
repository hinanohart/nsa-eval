# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our standards

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Take responsibility for mistakes; apologize and learn from them.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the reproducibility of the published numbers.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report incidents by opening a confidential issue tagged `coc` or by email to the repository owner. Reports are handled privately and the reporter's identity is not disclosed.
